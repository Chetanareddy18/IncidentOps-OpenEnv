Why This Matters — The $400B Problem

🏦 Fintech Outage Crisis

Payment system outages cost fintechs $9,400/minute. A single SEV1 incident averages 68 minutes MTTR manually — that's $639K per incident. Companies like Stripe, Square, and Razorpay face 3-5 major incidents per month.

🤖 AI-Driven Resolution

IncidentOps trains AI agents to handle the critical first 15 minutes of any incident — triage, diagnosis, fix, communication — reducing MTTR by 70%. The agent learns through dense reward signals across 5 difficulty tiers with realistic cascading failure scenarios.

📊 Production-Ready Evaluation

Six-axis grading rubric evaluates resolution quality, speed, communication, business impact, diagnosis accuracy, and efficiency. Compatible with any LLM via the OpenEnv standard. Deploy as a benchmark or fine-tuning environment.
